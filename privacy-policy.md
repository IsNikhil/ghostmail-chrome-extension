# Privacy Policy for GhostMail Chrome Extension

GhostMail does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal user data.

The extension operates entirely within the user's browser and only performs its intended functionality. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

GhostMail does not use data for advertising, analytics, creditworthiness, tracking, or profiling.
